📖 About Dogs

Dogs are friendly, loyal, and intelligent animals that have lived with humans for thousands of years.

⭐ Quick Facts

Scientific Name: Canis lupus familiaris
Lifespan: 10–15 years
Diet: Omnivore
Speed: Up to 72 km/h

🐾 Amazing Facts

  • Dogs have an excellent sense of smell.
  • Every dog's nose print is unique.
  • Dogs can understand many commands.
  • They are one of the world's most popular pets.
🎉 Fun Fact: Dogs can smell about 40 times better than humans!
